Player Rating Lookup

Step 1 — Find your profile. Type the name of the player this account is for in the search box below. Once your profile appears, click "Use as My Profile" to save it to your account.

Step 2 — Calculate rating impacts. Then go to USCF Calculator in the menu. There you can add tournaments, add opponents and games within each tournament, and see instant W/L/D rating impacts after every game — progressively as you enter results.

Step 3 — Attach your scoresheets. Next to each game you'll see a 📎 paperclip icon. Click it to attach your notation — either by taking a photo of your scoresheet with your phone camera, or by uploading a PGN file directly.
